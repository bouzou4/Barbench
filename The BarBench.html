Modern & Contemporary

With clean lines and monochromatic colors, the Bar Bench is a stylish piece of contemporary furniture. But it's much more than a storage cabinet. Sit down, wheel around, share a drink. The Bar Bench brings a spark of fun to an office, and it brings co-workers together.

Stir Creativity

Good ideas come from anyone and anywhere. But it takes a team to make a good idea a great one, and the Bar Bench is a platform for employees to bring out each other's best. Make the Bar Bench a break from the cubicle or the center of your next meeting. The possibilities are endless.
